Code Showcase: Project Euler #11

This problem challenged me the most out of the 15 I've completed so far. The goal is to find the greatest product of four adjacent numbers in the same direction (up, down, left, right, or diagonally) in a 20x20 grid.

Reflection

"Once I had figured out the logic to find the greatest product in the grid vertically, it was simple enough to tweak the logic slightly in order for it to work for finding the greatest horizontal product. The challenge came upon attempting to find the greatest diagonal product. However, after thinking visually about how I could scan the diagonals in order to find the greatest product, I realised that I could combine the logic for the horizontal and vertical methods that I created, and again add some minor adjustments in order to find the greatest diagonal product. This problem demonstrated the importance of understanding your code to the most fundamental level, allowing you to solve harder and harder problems by building up from logic that you have already constructed."
